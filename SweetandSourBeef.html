Sweet and Sour Beef

How to prepare the best Sweet and Sour Beef

Welcome to the best Sweet and Sour Beef preparation! Here you will learn how to prepare the most delicious Sweet and Sour Beef once you follow the laid down instructions.

First things first, you can not prepare the finest Sweet and Sour Beef without ingredients. Below is a list of 'must-have' ingredients

ingredients

  • 1 pound cubed beef stew meat
  • 3 cups cold water
  • Â¾ cup vinegar
  • 9 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 9 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 5 tablespoons cornstarch

steps

  1. Step 1: Heat a deep skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir beef in hot skillet until completely browned, 7 to 10 minutes. Transfer beef to a slow cooker.
  2. Step 2: Whisk water, vinegar, soy sauce, brown sugar, and cornstarch together until smooth; pour over the beef.
  3. Step 3: Cook on Low until the beef is tender and the sauce is thick, 6 to 8 hours.
